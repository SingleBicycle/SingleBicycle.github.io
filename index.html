Zihao (Zack) Zhu

I am an incoming Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Texas A&M University, advised by Prof. Zhiwen Fan. I received my B.S. in Computer Science from Texas A&M University, where I worked with Prof. Zhengzhong Tu.

My research aims to develop trustworthy and uncertainty-aware AI systems that can perceive, reconstruct, and reason about the visual world, with applications in 3D reconstruction, image restoration, generation, and multimodal understanding.
